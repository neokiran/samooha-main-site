Crafting A New Legacy

Samooha, the Hyderabad-based real estate company, was established with the idea of introducing aesthetic living at an affordable price. We understand the value of quality, and we respect the plan of our clientele and provide services accordingly. Samooha holds an experienced, professional, and dedicated team of talent to deliver optimum design satisfaction to the clients while balancing ever-evolving demands and emerging requirements.

We promise to offer lavish designs built-in sophisticated landscape layouts while ensuring comfort. Our product/service lines include construction, loan and registration assistance, and resale. Our exceptionally skilled team never compromises on work to deliver quality and hence acknowledged on lavish living experiences by ET Excellence Awards 2020-21 and awarded the team with Excellence in Affordable Plotting Development award.

To deliver the best of quality, we at Samooha Projects guard the project site with all-around compound walls to ensure the safety and security of the residing community. Our projects are facilitated with a well-curated play area and a recreational slot for children and senior citizens. Blacktop roads with avenue plantation and added Vaastu compliment plots speak about our keen eye on details.

We entrust the projects with zero compromises to tailor-made edifices at affordable prices, thus helping the company gain business through happy clients' references. Samooha Projects achieved appreciation for best-in-class services and dedication to meet client expectations.
