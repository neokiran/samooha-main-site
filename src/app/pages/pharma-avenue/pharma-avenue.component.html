Hyderabad Pharma City

Hyderabad Pharma City

Hyderabad Pharma City, is the "world’s largest pharmaceuticals industrial park" being established near Hyderabad, India by the Government of Telangana. Spread over 19,000 acres, the Park is touted to be biggest of its kind industrial cluster for the pharmaceuticals, acting as a one-stop solution for pharmaceutical companies in manufacturing and development needs.

Amazon Data Center

Amazon Data Center

E-commerce major Amazon, a leader in cloud computing service with its Amazon Web Services (AWS), is considering setting up two data centres near Hyderabad with a total investment of ₹11,630 crore (around $1.6 billion). One of the data centres is being proposed with an investment of ₹5,809 crore on over 48 acres (1,94,293 sq metres) in Meerkhanpet village under Kandukur mandal in neighbouring Rangareddy district.

Yeliminedu Aerospace

Yeliminedu Aerospace

500 acres has been provided at Eliminedu in Ibrahimpatnam for setting up another aerospace park while two more parks will come up on the outskirts of the city here around the Outer Ring Road. Hyderabad has emerged as a major aerospace hub with several global aerospace companies and original equipment manufacturers, including the Lockheed Martin Corporation, GE Aviation and Safran, having established their plants in the city.
